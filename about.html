Purpose

This tool was designed for Massachusetts residents who rely on a public water system for their drinking water. Public water systems are required to provide residents with a Consumer Confidence Report (CCR) annually. This CCR details the results of all water testing done by a public water system in that year. Call or go online to your local water department to find out more.


About the data

Water data for MA public water systems retrieved from: https://eeaonline.eea.state.ma.us/Portal/#!/search/drinking-water

All Community Water Systems in Massachusetts that reported water testing to the State are included.
