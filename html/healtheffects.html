According to the Agency for Toxic Substances and Disease Registry (ATSDR), studies on PFAS have linked high levels of exposure with:

  • increased cholesterol levels
  • changes in liver enzymes
  • decreased vaccine response in children
  • increased risk of high blood pressure or pre-eclampsia in pregnant women
  • small decreases in infant birth weights
  • increased risk of kidney or testicular cancer

    • If you are concerned about your health, ATSDR created a guide for talking to your doctor about your exposure.
